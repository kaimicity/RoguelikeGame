# ReadMe
1.After entering the name, press ENTER to start the game;
2.Use direction keys to move or use 'A', 'D' and 'S' to turn left right or back and use 'W' to move forwards to the direction orientating;\
3.Use ENTER to open treasures and collect chips ;
3.Press 'I' to open menus and 'M' to close menus;
4.In menus/battle, use direction keys to switch options/menus, use ENTER and BACKSPACE to choose and 
 unselect
5.In equipment menu, press 'D' to discharge a equipment;
